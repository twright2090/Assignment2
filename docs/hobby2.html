Reading

My love for reading was reignited from boredom. Two years ago, I was at home with nothing to do and desperate to try something new.
I had some friends who were into reading at the time and I've asked them to suggest me something that they were currently into.
I started to read the novel for myself and I would go on to buy and read more books. Now, it seems like I have too many books to read.
A writer that inspired me to become more invested into reading has to be Darius Foroux
